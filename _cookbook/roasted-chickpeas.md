## Pantry

- [ ] 16 oz can of chickpeas (I like STO low sodium)

### Seasoning options

* smoked paprika, garlic+onion powder, salt
* tajin (+lime zest)
* ask chatgpt

## Instructions

 - [ ] oven at 400-425
 - [ ] drain + dry chickpeas
 - [ ] toss with oil + seasonings (go hard here; more than most roasted veggies)
 - [ ] roast ~30 minutes tossing midway
